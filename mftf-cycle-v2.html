What this is
A year-round creative music program for incarcerated individuals. Participants move through a seasonal arc — holiday tours build relationships, fall residencies deliver coursework, spring produces a full musical theatre work, and summer culminates in a premiere livestreamed from Lincoln Center to all partner facilities.
Current program structure
  • 7-week program: 5-day in-person residency + 6-week remote course
  • Taught by San Francisco Conservatory of Music (SFCM) faculty
  • Certificates of Completion from PMHU & SFCM
  • 2,400+ participants to date
  • Primary partner: Five Keys (CA) · Expanding: CT, NV
Core program team
  • Dana — artistic lead, co-writes the musical, leads acting instruction
  • Michael Shoff — co-writer, present through full 4-week production
  • SFCM faculty — remote course instruction
  • Musician teams + TAs embedded per genre track
